The Problem

Most reminders are too vague—creating stress, indecision, and stalled progress. When tasks lack clarity, it's hard to know where to start or what to do next.

Our Solution

Next Step turns unclear to‑dos into clear actions. By integrating seamlessly with Apple Reminders and using on‑device intelligence, it transforms “Work on project” into specific, actionable next steps.
